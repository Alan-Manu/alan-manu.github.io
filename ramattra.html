Overwatch 2 - Tank Heroes

Ramattra

Ramattra is a Tank hero in Overwatch 2. He was an omnic robot and the ex leader of an organisation called null sector, ramattra has 2 forms, an Omnic form and the Nemesis form, in the omnic form he has two abilities, one is Void Accelerator, he fires a stream of projecties at the enemy dealing damage.

Ramattra's abilities even include Void Barrier, which is used in his omnic form, targets a position on the ground and depolys a shield for a short amount of time to block incoming attacks, and Pummel, this ability transfroms him into nemesis mode where ramattra gains health and punches, creating a wave of piercing energy with every swing. He can even block attacks in nemesis form.

Another ability that is Ravenous Vortex, which fires a sphere of energy that creates a slowing vortex on the ground, damaging enemies while pulling them downward.

Ramattra's ultimate ability, Annihilation, where ramattra enters nemesis form and creates a deadly swarm, where it lashes out at nearby enemies, lasts longer when damaging enemies.
