Overwatch 2 - Tank Heroes

Ramattra

Ramattra is a Tank hero in Overwatch 2. He was an omnic robot and the ex-leader of an organization called Null Sector. Ramattra has 2 forms, an Omnic form and the Nemesis form. In the omnic form, he has two abilities, one is Void Accelerator, where he fires a stream of projectiles at the enemy, dealing damage.

Ramattra's abilities also include Void Barrier, which is used in his omnic form, targeting a position on the ground and deploying a shield for a short amount of time to block incoming attacks, and Pummel, which transforms him into nemesis mode where Ramattra gains health and punches, creating a wave of piercing energy with every swing. He can even block attacks in nemesis form.

Another ability is Ravenous Vortex, which fires a sphere of energy that creates a slowing vortex on the ground, damaging enemies while pulling them downward.

Ramattra's ultimate ability, Annihilation, allows Ramattra to enter nemesis form and create a deadly swarm, lashing out at nearby enemies. The ultimate lasts longer when damaging enemies.
