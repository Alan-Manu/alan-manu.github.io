Overwatch 2 - Tank Heroes

Reinhardt

Reinhardt is a stalwart Tank hero in Overwatch 2. He is a formidable presence on the battlefield, protecting his teammates and leading the charge. Reinhardt's primary weapon Rocket Hammer is a Rocket powered hammer that is swung by reinhardt and deals damage at close range to enemies

Reinhardt's abilities include Charge, where reinhardt charges forward, if an enemy is caught while he is going forward, is killed if reinhardt smashes into a wall, his other ability Fire Strike, creates a fiery projectile that damages the enemy from afar if hit by it.

Another ability that is Barrier Field, creates a energy shield blocking incoming attacks and helps his team to push enemies.

Reinhardt's ultimate ability, Earthshatter, uses reinhardt's rocket hammer to hit the ground and deal splash damage, if enemies are within the splash area, they a stunned for a short duration enabling reinhardt to deal damage or kill the enemy.
