Overwatch 2 - Tank Heroes

Winston

Winston is a genetically engineered gorilla and a Tank hero in Overwatch 2. He excels at making space for his team, using his abilities and his ultimate. His primary weapon Tesla Canon is a short-range weapon that fires electricity, damaging enemies using his weapon and doing chip damage.

Winston's abilities include Jump Pack, where Winston leaps into the air, slamming the enemies, displacing and damaging them. He has another ability called Barrier Projector, where Winston places a protective energy dome which protects himself and his enemies from incoming enemies.

Winston's ultimate Primal Rage, where Winston goes into rage turning red and he can only punch enemies and jump around creating space for his team to attack.
