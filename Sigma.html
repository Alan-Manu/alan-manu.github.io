Overwatch 2 - Tank Heroes

Sigma

Sigma is a floating Tank hero in Overwatch 2. He excels at disrupting the enemy team, absorbing damage, and for his ultimate. His primary weapon HyperSpheres is a medium-ranged two energy spheres that deals damage to enemies and can be bounced off of walls and the ground to reach angles that are hard to peek from.

Sigma's abilities include Kinetic Grasp, where sigma absorbs all damage, turning it into exrta health, his other ability Accretion, sigma gathers a mass of debris and can throw it at an enemy to knock them down.

His other ability Experimental barrier he creates a floating barrier that can block incoming damage. this barrier can also be recalled and redeployed after a certain duration.

Sigma's ultimate Gravitic Flux, manipulates gravity to lift enemies up in the air to then slam them to the ground either damaging or killing them.
