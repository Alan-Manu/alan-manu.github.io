Overwatch 2 - Tank Heroes

Wrecking Ball

Wrecking Ball a.k.a Hammond is a genetically engineered hamster and a ball Tank hero in Overwatch 2. Wrecking ball is an intelligent hamster who controls a quadrupedal robot mecha that can transform into a high-speed wrecking ball equipped with a grappling hook. His primary weapon Quad Cannons is a medium-range automatic weapon.

Wrecking Ball's abilities include Grappling Claw, where wrecking ball uses it to gain speed and rapidly swing around the area, giving high-speed collisions and damage to enemies. He has another ability called Roll, this ability makes wrecking ball transform into a ball, increasing his momentum.

Another ability is Piledriver, where wrecking ball slams into the ground, launching enemies upwards and damaging them. Another ability is Adaptive Shield, which gives wrecking ball a temporary shield, with the amount increasing with the number of enemies around him.

Wrecking Ball's ultimate is Minefield. Wrecking ball drops a field of proximity mines that instantly kill enemies who are in their range.
