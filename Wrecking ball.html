Wrecking Ball

Wrecking Ball a.k.a Hammond is a genetically engineered hamster and a ball Tank hero in Overwatch 2. Wrecking ball is an intelligent hamster who controls a quadrupedal robot mecha that can transform into a high-speed wrecking ball equipped with a grappling hook. His primary weapon Quad Cannons is a medium range automatic weapon.

Wrecking Balls's abilities include Grappling Claw, where wrecking ball uses it to gain speed and rapidly swing around the area, gives high speed collisions and damge to enemies. He has another ability called Roll, this ability makes wrecking ball transform into a ball making his increasing his momentum.

Another ability is Piledriver wrecking ball slams into the ground, launching enemies upwards and damaging them. Another ability that is Adaptive Shield gives wrecking ball temporary shield but the amount increaes with the number of enemies around him.

Wrecking Ball's ultimate Minefield, wrecking ball drops a field of proximity mines where if enemies are in it, will kill them instantly.
