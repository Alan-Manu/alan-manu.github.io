Doomfist

Doomfist is a Tank hero in Overwatch 2. His Real Name is Akande Ogundimu. He is a highly mobile hero with powerful melee attacks. His primary weapon is the Hand Cannon, which allows him to deal damage from short to medium range.

Doomfist's abilities include Rocket Punch, a devastating charge attack that can knock enemies back and deal massive damage upon impact, and Power Block, which blocks incoming attacks, empowering his Rocket Punch to deal extra damgage than usual. Siesmic Slam , Where doomfist leap into the air and smashes into the ground delaing damage to enemies in his line of sight.

Doomfist's ultimate ability, Meteor Strike , allows him to leap into the air and crash down, dealing damage to all enemies in the impact area and creating chaos on the battlefield.
