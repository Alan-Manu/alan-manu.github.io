Overwatch 2 - Tank Heroes

Zarya

Zarya is a tank that heavily focuses on defensive abilites. Her primary weapon Particle Cannon is a short ranged linear beam weapon that can damage enemies.

Zarya's abilities include Particle Barrier, where she deploys a barrier she protects herself from incoming damage and increases the damge dealt using her passive. Her other shield abilityProjected Barrier, zarya uses her barrier to protect allies, this also works the same way as her particle barrier where damage received makes the damage output of her particle cannon higher

Zarya's ultimate Graviton Surge, zarya deploys a gravity well that pulls enemies towards it and makes it so that they cant use their abilities while being pulled.
