Overwatch 2 - Tank Heroes

Roadhog

Roadhog is a powerful Tank hero in Overwatch 2. He excels at disrupting the enemy team, absorbing damage, and isolating key targets. His primary weapon Scrap Gun is a short-range shotgun that deals significant damage to enemies in close range.

Roadhog's abilities include Hook, Roadhog throws a chain with a hook attached, which can reel in and stun an enemy, bringing them closer for a quick elimination. His other ability Take A Breather allows him to heal himself by inhaling a gas-like substance, restoring a portion of his health over a short duration.

Roadhog's ultimate ability is called Whole Hog. It unleashes a barrage of scrap projectiles that knock back enemies and deal massive damage.
