Overwatch 2 - Tank Heroes

D.Va

D.Va is a Tank hero in Overwatch 2. Her Real Name is Hana Song. She pilots a state-of-the-art mech suit equipped with twin Fusion Cannons allow her to deal high damage at close range. When her mech is destroyed, D.Va ejects and continues to fight on foot using her Light Gun.

D.Va's abilities include Defense Matrix, which can absorb incoming projectiles and provide protection to her teammates, and Boosters , which allow her to fly through the air and collide with enemies, dealing damage and knocking them back.

D.Va's ultimate ability, Self-Destruct , ejects her mech and sets it to self-destruct, causing a massive explosion that can devastate the enemy team if used strategically.

D.va also has another ultimate ability, Call-Mech where, after the ejected D.Va deals a certain amount of damage, filling the ultimate, enables D.Va to call her mech back to get a second chance at fighting.
