Junker Queen

Junker Queen is a Tank hero in Overwatch. As the fierce leader of the Junkers, she brings chaos and destruction to the battlefield. Junker Queen wields a Scattergun, pump action shotgun which deals spread damage to enemies, useful in close range battles.

In addition to her Scattergun, Junker Queen has various other abilities. Her Jagged blade, which she uses as a throwing knife deals damage to enemy and also does wound damage, which is dealing damage over time. She also has another ability called Commanding Shout , where she gains and gives temporary health and speed to her and her aliies by shouting. she even has an ability called Carnage where she wields an axe that can damage multiple enemies by swinging the axe at them at close range. this axe also delas wond damage.

Her ultimate ability Rampage, makes her charg forward damagin enemies and woundign them overtime, also prevents them from being healed for a short duration of time. she is one of the few tank heroes to have a passive ability, which is called Adrenaline Rush where junker queen heals from the wound damage dealt to enemies.
