Overwatch Role: Tank

Junker Queen

Junker Queen is a Tank hero in Overwatch. As the fierce leader of the Junkers, she brings chaos and destruction to the battlefield. Junker Queen wields a Scattergun, a pump-action shotgun which deals spread damage to enemies, useful in close-range battles.

In addition to her Scattergun, Junker Queen has various other abilities. Her Jagged Blade, which she uses as a throwing knife, deals damage to enemies and inflicts wound damage, causing damage over time. She also has another ability called Commanding Shout, where she gains and grants temporary health and speed to herself and her allies by shouting. She even has an ability called Carnage where she wields an axe that can damage multiple enemies by swinging it at close range. This axe also deals wound damage.

Her ultimate ability Rampage allows her to charge forward, damaging enemies and inflicting wound damage over time, while also preventing them from being healed for a short duration. She is one of the few tank heroes to have a passive ability called Adrenaline Rush where Junker Queen heals from the wound damage dealt to enemies.
