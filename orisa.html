Overwatch 2 - Tank Heroes

Orisa

Orisa is a Tank hero in Overwatch 2. She is a robotic centaur armed with a powerful projectile cannon, the Augmented Fusion Driver, which allows her to deal sustained damage from a distance.

Orisa's abilities include Fortify, which reduces incoming damage and makes her immune to crowd control effects, and Energy Javelin, when thrown, can stun and damage enemies in their tracks, more effective if the enemies hit a wall.

Another ability that is Javelin spin, which stops incoming attacks and melee attacks while pushing enemies and increasing movement speed.

Orisa's ultimate ability, Terra surge, Sweeps the enemies towards her, gaining the effects of fortify and charging up a surge of damage to be unleashed at the enemy, turning the tide of battle.
